Our story
Started simple, built on trust

We're a small, independent car business based in South Florida. We source pre-owned vehicles and sell them at prices that make sense — no dealership markup, no pressure tactics.

We share what we know about each vehicle upfront — mileage, condition notes, any visible wear. We encourage every buyer to come see the car in person, ask questions, and make the call themselves. That's how we like to do business.

We're building this from the ground up, and every customer matters to us. You deal with us directly, get straight answers, and leave knowing exactly what the deal is.

What we do
How we find our cars

We source vehicles from auctions, private sellers, and local listings. We look for solid economy cars and compact SUVs — Toyotas, Hondas, Hyundais, Kias — the kind of cars that are reliable, affordable to maintain, and easy to resell.

Before any car goes up for sale, we go through it. We note cosmetic issues and any known mechanical details in the listing. We don't deal in flood-damaged or frame-damaged vehicles — ever.
